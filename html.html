Hello Visitor,

There are so many places that you can visit in Saudi Arabia! Saudi Arabia is actually well known for its diverse landscape and every place is worth giving a visit. There are some places that attract a large number of people like you. For example, there are some many majestic mosques, wonderful museums and more. To add on, some places symbolize history from the past and some places are modern as well.

Raghadan Park in Baha

Hover over the image to learn about this location.

Raghadan Park in Baha is a verdant paradise for anyone who admires nature. Spanning a vast 20,000 square meters, this park offers a refreshing escape from the daily grind with its lush green spaces and dense forest. The park has a 700-meter main walkway and contains 18,000 square meters of greenery which provides ample space for relaxation. There is a 30-meter waterfall, an interactive fountain, and a 25-meter decorative pedestrian bridge. Also, for anyone who loves a bit of adventure, the park features a 500-meter walkway covered in natural stone that is also connected to some waterfalls. Raghadan Park doesn’t just offer natural beauty; it’s also a place to savour delicious treats by visiting a variety of cafés, which are perfect for grabbing a coffee or a snack.

Al-Haram Mosque

Hover over the image to learn about this location.

This Mosque is located in the sacred city of Mecca in Saudi Arabia and Muslims from around the globe often travel to the al-Haram Mosque during the last month of the Islamic calendar. It is considered a duty for Muslims to undertake this pilgrimage at least once during their lifetime. Only Muslims can come here, so people who aren't Muslims are not allowed to come visit the al-Haram Mosque. This Mosque was built a very long time ago by Prophet Ibrahim and this Mosque is open for 24 hours. This Mosque also covers an area of 356,800 sq metres.

The Red Sea Mall

Hover over the image to learn about this location.

The Red Sea mall is a shopping center that is located in Jeddah’s suburb in the North and this mall is one of the largest malls. This mall is a great place to spend time with your family while shopping, watching a movie at a cinema and playing games at the arcade. This mall is packed with many international brands and stores where you can buy clothes, electronics, footwear, cosmetics, home goods, and more. This mall has extensive amenities, which include restaurants, international brands shops, kid-friendly areas, and prayer rooms. The Red Sea mall has the biggest indoor water fountain and has the largest glass-covered area in Saudi Arabia.

Al Faisaliah Center

Hover over the image to learn about this location.

An iconic place to visit in the city Riyadh in Saudi Arabia is called the Al Faisaliah Center, and this place displays the modernization of the city. This place is a 247 meters tall tower, which makes it the fourth tallest building in Saudi Arabia. The tower also features a dine-in restaurant called the Globe that offers a delectable European savory experience with a 360-degree view of the city. This eccentric skyscraper also has a mall in its basement, so you can go shopping here.
